The Lake Shawnee Amusement Park and Its Horrifying History

Founded in 1926, the Lake Shawnee Amusement Park swiftly became a popular family spot. However, whether due to a cursed history, or pure misfortune, the park became home to the deaths of a total of six visitors, including two young children. Join me on this video journey as we learn of the terrifying history behind the now haunted, Lake Shawnee Amusement Park.
