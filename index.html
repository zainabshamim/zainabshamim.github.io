The Lyrebirds Podcast: Don't F*ck With Cats

20 mins

Social media popularity is not a rare goal for people to have. Luka Magnotta, cat killer turned murderer, was hunted down by a group of strangers that connected with each other through something as accessible as Facebook. Within this podcast, my peers and I discuss Magnotta, and the infamous Netflix docuseries centered around him: "Don't Fuck with Cats".
